Issues Identified

Bottom nav has too many tabs - Client has 7 tabs, Assistant has 6 tabs. This causes horizontal scroll which confuses users.
Payment feature not discoverable - Clients can't see payment history or access past invoices easily.
No payment setup CTA for assistants - Assistants need to configure Venmo/Zelle but the link is hidden.
